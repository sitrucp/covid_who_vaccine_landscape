WHO Draft landscape of COVID-19 Candidate Vaccines

The WHO creates a regularly updated PDF document named Draft landscape of COVID-19 candidate vaccines which contains all COVID-19 vaccine candidates currently being developed and their status. AWS Textract was used to extract this PDF document's table data into csv format. Read more about how this was done.
